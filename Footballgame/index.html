Click to Play

W - Move forward
S - Move backward
A - Move left
D - Move right
Mouse - Look around
Left Click - Kick the ball
E - Change Shot Type
F - Heel Flick Dash
G - Super Shot (1 min cooldown)
ESC - Exit pointer lock

Idle
Press and hold W to run
Release W to return to idle
Hold left mouse button to charge kick
Release to execute kick

Game Controls

Movement

W Move forward
S Move backward
A Move left
D Move right
Mouse Look around

Ball Control

LMB Kick ball (hold to charge)
E Change shot type
F Heel Flick Dash
Space Pause/Resume game

Special

M Show/hide controls
F Heel Flick Dash
G Super Shot (60s cooldown)
ESC Exit game

Shot Types

Power Shot: Maximum velocity
Finesse Shot: Accurate placement
Curved Shot: Bends around defenders
Lob Shot: High arc over obstacles
